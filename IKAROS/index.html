About the Story

IKAROS story begins far distance future from now in a deep underground fortified city, inside the Closed Ecological Systems littered with the ruins of man’s Golden Age.

A massive dungeon was surrounded the city. No one knows when it built or what its end are, but only that it’s preventing them to go to outside world.Meanwhile, power used to maintain the city start running out, lead to importance of finding new source of energy in underground.

We are “IKAROS” tasked with defending the remains of humanity, discovering the possible source of energy and –eventually- overcome the endless dungeon.
